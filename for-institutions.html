Partnering With Institutions That Shape the Future of Medicine

MD Scholars partners with schools, universities, and training programs to deliver structured research mentorship at scale — with flexible enrollment options and institutional support.

Partnership Options

We work with institutions across the medical education spectrum. Whether you're looking for cohort-based training, one-time workshops, or research symposiums, we offer customizable solutions.

Cohort-Based Programs

Enroll groups of students in structured 12-16 week research tracks with dedicated mentorship, group seminars, and institutional reporting. Ideal for medical schools, residency programs, and pre-med departments.

Research Workshops

Single or multi-session seminars on topics like biostatistics, scientific writing, research design, and ethics. Perfect for career fairs, grand rounds, or faculty development.

Student Research Symposiums

Host a research showcase where students present their work. We provide judging, mentoring, and certificates. Great for celebrating scholarly achievement across your institution.

What We Offer

Group Pricing

Enrollment discount for institutions with 20+ students. Custom pricing based on cohort size, duration, and institutional needs.

📊

KPI Dashboard

Real-time tracking of student progress, mentorship metrics, research outcomes, and scholarly deliverables. Transparent reporting for institutional leadership.

🎓

Pilot Program

Live Info Sessions

Schedule presentations for your faculty, students, and leadership. Q&A sessions with our team and alumni from your institution.

🎤

Seminars & Webinars

Guest lectures on research methodology, publication strategy, residency competitiveness, and career development. Customized for your audience.

🤝

Career Fairs

Connect your students with physician-scientists. Networking, mentorship matching, and direct pathways to research opportunities.
