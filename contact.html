Frequently Asked Questions

How long is each program?

Our programs range from 6 weeks to 12 months depending on the track. High school and pre-med programs are typically shorter, while medical student and resident programs offer longer engagement options.

Do I need prior research experience?

No! We welcome scholars at all levels. Whether you're just starting your research journey or looking to deepen existing skills, we have a program designed for you.

Is this program online?

Yes, all our programs are delivered online, making them accessible to students worldwide. Our flexible scheduling accommodates busy academic and clinical calendars.

How do I know if I qualify?

We accept scholars from diverse backgrounds and experience levels. Visit our Programs page to review specific eligibility criteria for your track, or contact us with questions.
