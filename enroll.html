Frequently Asked Questions

When do I pay?

Payment is due upon enrollment confirmation. If you select a payment plan, you'll receive an invoice for the first installment immediately, with subsequent payments due on agreed dates. You'll have 48 hours to complete payment before your spot is released.

What if I need to cancel?

We offer a 7-day full refund policy from the date of enrollment. After that, please contact us to discuss deferment to a future cohort or a partial refund, depending on timing and circumstances.

Do you offer scholarships?

Yes! MD Scholars offers need-based scholarships and payment plans for qualified applicants. During the application process, you can request financial assistance. Our team will work with you to make the program accessible.
